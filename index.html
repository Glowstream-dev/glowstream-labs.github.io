How Glowstream works

A simple loop: creators publish, fans engage, offers convert, and payouts stay transparent.

1) Create + publish

Post in feeds, open a store, set your tiers, and define what requests you accept.

2) Offer-driven engagement

Fans can tip, subscribe, buy from stores, or propose offers for custom interactions.

3) Payouts + sharing

Circles can split revenue across performers; platform rules stay clear and consistent.

Compliance by design (clear rails)

Glowstream separates experiences by user choice and payment eligibility. Mainstream commerce stays on eligible checkout; restricted experiences (if ever enabled) use separate providers.

Door A: Mainstream commerce

Default experience designed to stay friendly for broad audiences and eligible payment rails.

  • Subscriptions (general)
  • Tips / support
  • Stores & merch

Door B: 18+ opt-in (future)

If enabled later, it will require explicit consent, age gate, disclosures, and a separate approved provider + checkout.

  • Separate provider (e.g., CCBill)
  • Separate disclosures + age verification
  • Separate checkout + content separation
